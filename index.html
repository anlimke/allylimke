Ally Limke, computer science PhD student studying UX and computing education.

Biography

I am in the 4th year of the CS PhD program at NC State. Over the course of the program, I have had the pleasure to contribute to and lead a range of research projects in the Game2Learn lab. My research interests are in User Experience and K-12 computing education. Additionally, I mentor K-12 teachers and undergraduates in computing education research. Through my role the STARS Computing Corps Vice President at NC State, I organize computing outreach opportunities at the undergraduate, high school, and middle school levels.
